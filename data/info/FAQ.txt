FAQ [Frequently Asked Questions]
1. What are the mandatory steps that I have to follow to become a valid applicant?
A. The first step of the CSS online registration process is to click on the "Click here for Registration" button available on the homepage of the website www.admissions.keralauniversity.ac.in. After that, the candidate has to provide basic details such as Name, Date of Birth, Gender, Email ID, and Mobile Number. Once the required details are entered, an OTP will be sent for mobile number verification. After successful verification, a unique Application Number will be provided.
B. After generating the Application number the candidate should login with the Application number and Password. Date of Birth is the password by default (DD/MM/YYYY)
C. The candidate should follow the 5 steps of the online registration process as mentioned in the prospectus.
D. DO NOT SEND HARD COPIES OF ONLINE APPLICATIONS TO THE UNIVERSITY.
2. Is payment through University cash counter accepted?
No, only payment made through online mode is accepted. The payment can be made either using Net Banking, Debit Card or Credit Card.
3. Is payment as DD/Cheque accepted?
No
4. I forgot my password and I am not able to enter my Home Page. What should I do now?
You can contact the helpline numbers provided in the admissions website.
